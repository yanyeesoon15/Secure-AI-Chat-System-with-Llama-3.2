2. WORKING HOURS & ATTENDANCE
================================================================================

Standard Working Hours:
- Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- Lunch Break: 1:00 PM - 2:00 PM (1 hour)
- Total Working Hours: 40 hours per week

Flexible Working Arrangements:
- Core Hours: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
- Flex Time: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM
- Minimum 40 hours per week
- Manager approval required for flexible arrangements

Remote Work Policy:
- Up to 2 days per week (subject to manager approval)
- Remote work request must be submitted 24 hours in advance
- Internet subsidy of RM 100/month provided
- Must maintain connectivity and responsiveness

Attendance Expectations:
- All employees must clock in/out using the HR system
- Late arrival (more than 15 minutes) must be reported
- 3 late arrivals in a month = verbal warning
- 5 late arrivals in a month = written warning
- Unauthorized absence = disciplinary action

4. CODE OF CONDUCT
================================================================================

Professional Behavior:
- Treat all colleagues with respect and courtesy
- Avoid discriminatory language or actions
- Maintain a positive and collaborative attitude
- Report any misconduct to HR

Confidentiality:
- Protect company confidential information
- Do not share internal documents externally
- Secure your computer and documents
- Report any security breaches immediately

Conflict of Interest:
- Disclose any potential conflicts of interest
- Do not engage in activities that compete with the company
- Avoid accepting gifts from clients/vendors above RM 100
- Report any conflicts to your manager or HR

Anti-Harassment Policy:
- Zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination
- Report any incidents to HR immediately
- All reports will be investigated confidentially
- No retaliation against reporters

Email & Internet Usage:
- Company email is for work purposes
- Personal use should be limited
- Do not share passwords
- Report suspicious emails to IT

10. GRIEVANCE PROCEDURE
================================================================================

Step 1: Informal Resolution
- Discuss with your immediate manager
- Attempt to resolve directly
- Document the issue

Step 2: Formal Complaint
- Submit formal grievance to HR
- Complete grievance form
- Provide supporting documents
- HR investigation initiated

Step 3: Investigation
- HR conducts investigation
- Witness interviews conducted
- Evidence reviewed
- Findings documented

Step 4: Resolution
- Outcome communicated in writing
- Actions or recommendations provided
- Appeal process explained
- Follow-up schedule established

Appeals:
- Submit appeal within 7 days
- Appeal to HR Director
- Final decision by CEO
- All decisions are final
