8. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: When can I enroll in the health insurance?
A: On your first day of employment. Coverage begins immediately.

Q: How many days of annual leave can I carry forward?
A: Up to 10 days. Must be used by March 31 of following year.

Q: Can I work from overseas?
A: Up to 4 weeks per year. Must be approved by manager. Need to maintain productivity.

Q: Is the gym membership mandatory?
A: No. It's a voluntary benefit. You can opt-out anytime.

Q: How do I request leave?
A: Through the HR portal. Approval within 24 hours.

Q: What's the overtime policy?
A: Manager's approval required. Paid as time-off in lieu.

Q: Are there wellness activities available?
A: Yes, we have weekly yoga, meditation sessions, and monthly health challenges.

Q: What happens to my unused leave when I leave?
A: Unused leave will be paid out in your final paycheck.
