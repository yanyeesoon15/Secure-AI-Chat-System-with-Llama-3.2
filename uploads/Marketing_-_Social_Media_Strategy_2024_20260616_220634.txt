SOCIAL MEDIA STRATEGY & CONTENT CALENDAR 2024

1. PLATFORM STRATEGY

Facebook (Primary Platform)
- Target Audience: 25-45 years old, professionals
- Posting Frequency: 3-4 times per week
- Best Posting Times: Monday-Thursday, 10am-2pm
- Content Mix: 50% educational, 30% promotional, 20% community engagement
- Goal: Brand awareness and community building

Instagram (Secondary Platform)
- Target Audience: 18-35 years old, visually-oriented
- Posting Frequency: 4-5 times per week
- Best Posting Times: Monday-Friday, 8am-12pm, 6pm-9pm
- Content Mix: 60% visual storytelling, 25% product showcase, 15% behind-the-scenes
- Goal: Brand perception and engagement

LinkedIn (Professional Platform)
- Target Audience: 30-55 years old, B2B professionals
- Posting Frequency: 2-3 times per week
- Best Posting Times: Tuesday-Thursday, 8am-10am, 12pm-1pm
- Content Mix: 70% thought leadership, 20% company news, 10% job postings
- Goal: B2B leads and talent attraction

TikTok (Emerging Platform)
- Target Audience: 16-25 years old, trend-focused
- Posting Frequency: 3-4 times per week
- Best Posting Times: Daily, 7pm-10pm
- Content Mix: 70% entertaining, 20% educational, 10% behind-the-scenes
- Goal: Brand awareness among younger audience

5. BRAND VOICE GUIDELINES

Do's:
✓ Use active voice and engaging language
✓ Keep posts concise (40-80 characters for headlines)
✓ Include clear calls-to-action
✓ Tag relevant partners and customers
✓ Use hashtags (3-5 per post)

Don'ts:
✗ Use jargon or technical terms without explanation
✗ Post without visual elements
✗ Ignore negative comments (respond professionally)
✗ Share competitor content
✗ Post during off-peak hours

6. CONTENT CREATION PROCESS

Step 1: Strategy Planning (Monthly)
- Review previous month's performance
- Set monthly goals and themes
- Identify key dates and events

Step 2: Content Development (2 Weeks Before)
- Create content ideas based on themes
- Develop visual assets (images, videos, graphics)
- Write captions and copy

Step 3: Review and Approval (1 Week Before)
- Submit to manager for review
- Check for brand alignment
- Ensure all assets are ready

Step 4: Scheduling (3 Days Before)
- Schedule posts using management tools
- Set up monitoring for engagement
- Prepare responses for common questions

Step 5: Posting and Monitoring (Day Of)
- Post at scheduled times
- Monitor engagement in first 2 hours
- Respond to comments and messages
