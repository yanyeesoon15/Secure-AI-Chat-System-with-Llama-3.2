- Impact: 25% chance
- Mitigation: 10% contingency budget (RM 85,000)
- Response: Real-time budget monitoring, weekly reviews

Risk 2: Competitor Response
- Impact: 30% chance
- Mitigation: Monitor competitor activity, adjust strategy
- Response: Quick-response team ready for adjustments

Risk 3: Economic Downturn
- Impact: 15% chance
- Mitigation: Flexible campaign structure
- Response: Shift to value-based messaging, offer flexible payments

Risk 4: Team Capacity
- Impact: 20% chance

Risk 5: Technology Issues
- Impact: 10% chance
- Mitigation: Robust testing, backup systems
- Response: IT support on standby, alternative platforms ready
